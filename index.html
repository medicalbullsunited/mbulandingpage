Medical Bulls United was founded on a simple but powerful belief: the road to medicine should not be a competition. We exist to dismantle the toxic competitiveness that plagues pre-med culture and replace it with collaboration, community, and shared growth.

We are a unified umbrella organization bringing together international students and domestic students — bridging cultural and linguistic divides — because the diversity of our backgrounds makes us stronger physicians.

At MBU, every member has access to the same shadowing, research, and volunteering opportunities regardless of their pathway. We rise together.

What We Offer

Three Pillars of Growth

Every MBU member gains access to opportunities across all three pillars from the moment they join — no gatekeeping, no barriers.

🏥
Shadowing
Gain hands-on exposure to clinical environments. MBU coordinates shadowing opportunities with physicians across a range of specialties — giving you the hours and experience medical schools look for.
🔬
Research
Connect with research opportunities across departments through M.B.U.R. (Ember) — our dedicated research program led by Research Director Keyur Patel. From oncology to psychiatry, we'll find your fit.
🤝
Volunteering
Give back to the community while building your application. MBU partners with local organizations to provide meaningful volunteer experiences that align with your passion for medicine and service.

M.B.U.R. — Ember

Medical Bulls United
Research Program

M.B.U.R. (pronounced Ember) is MBU's dedicated undergraduate research initiative. We connect pre-med students with real research opportunities across multiple disciplines — building the academic profiles and scientific foundations that medical schools are looking for.

Whether you're just starting out or looking to deepen an existing research interest, M.B.U.R. is your gateway to meaningful scientific work as an undergraduate.

How It Works

Two Paths, One Mission

When you join MBU, you'll be placed into one of two pathways based on your background. Both pathways are deeply connected — all events, resources, and opportunities are shared across the entire MBU family.

GPP
Global Pre-Med Pathway

Designed for international students navigating the unique challenges of applying to U.S. medical schools — from credential evaluation and visa considerations to cultural adaptation and building a competitive profile from abroad.

GPP Leadership
  • Pedro Victor Souza Peres — GPP President
  • Khushi Shah — GPP Vice President
  • Areen Abid — Coordinator
  • Nadine Beguma — Coordinator
United
TPP
Traditional Pre-Med Pathway

For domestic students following the conventional U.S. pre-med route. TPP focuses on MCAT strategy, clinical hours, research, and building the well-rounded application profile required for U.S. allopathic and osteopathic medical schools.
